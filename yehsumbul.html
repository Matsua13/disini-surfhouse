Yeh Sumbul

Yeh Sumbul - Your Local Paradise

Welcome to Yeh Sumbul, a peaceful yet vibrant surf town where adventure and relaxation blend perfectly. Whether you're here for the waves or to soak in the beauty, there's so much to explore.

Nature & Adventure

Discover the natural wonders of Yeh Sumbul:

Hidden Waterfalls

Let us guide you to the secret waterfalls tucked away in the lush greenery – perfect for a refreshing dip or a stunning photo opportunity.

Mountains & Empty Beaches

Explore scenic routes by scooter or on foot to discover breathtaking mountain views and tranquil, empty beaches.

Rivers & Cliff Jumps

Experience the thrill of jumping off cliffs into cool, clear rivers – an adrenaline rush for the adventurous.

Local Dining & Nightlife

Experience the vibrant flavors and exciting nights of Yeh Sumbul:

Good Restaurants

From cozy warungs to stylish cafes with ocean views, enjoy authentic Indonesian cuisine in a relaxed setting.

Night Markets

Immerse yourself in the local culture with vibrant night markets offering delicious street food and handmade crafts.

Karaoke Nights

Unleash your inner rockstar and enjoy fun-filled karaoke nights that bring friends and families together.
