Disini - Your Bali Surf Escape

Welcome to Disini, your cozy surf home stay in Yeh Sumbul, Bali. We're building a community where travelers and surfers come together to experience the authentic Bali lifestyle.

Our surf coaching is already in full swing – perfect for beginners looking to catch their first wave or intermediate surfers aiming to level up. Our experienced local instructors guide you every step of the way.

We're opening our accommodations in Summer/Fall 2025, designed for pure comfort and a chill vibe. Whether you're here to surf, explore, or just relax, Disini is where you belong.
